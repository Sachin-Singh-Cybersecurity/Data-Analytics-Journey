# Level 6 — E-Commerce Business Analysis with matplotlib

## Project Overview
This project focuses on performing business analysis on an e-commerce dataset using Python, pandas, and matplotlib.

The project combines:
- data analysis
- visualization
- business insights
- trend interpretation

The objective was to analyze sales and profit trends across different business dimensions.

# Key Insights

- South and West regions showed strong sales performance.
- Technology category generated the highest profitability.
- Furniture category showed comparatively weaker profit contribution.
- Monthly sales showed fluctuations indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- Business performance varied across regions and product categories.
